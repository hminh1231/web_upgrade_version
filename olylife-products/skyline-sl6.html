Core Technologies

1

Smart Composite Chip

A sophisticated composite chip containing magnetic micro-units, far-infrared ceramic crystals, and a graphene interface. Together, these elements support circulation, ease tension, promote gentle warmth, and help the body maintain comfort during the menstrual cycle.

2

Graphene Wellness Layer

Graphene amplifies far-infrared energy to relax muscles and encourage smoother flow while naturally inhibiting bacteria and neutralizing odors. The result is a clean, balanced environment without chemical fragrances or irritation.

3

Bamboo Fiber Surface

The top layer is crafted from premium bamboo fibers—soft, breathable, naturally antibacterial, and exceptionally skin-friendly. It regulates moisture, reduces heat accumulation, and prevents the stickiness commonly associated with synthetic materials.

4

Ultra-Thin Absorbent Core

Engineered at a remarkably thin 0.8mm, the absorbent core delivers rapid three-second absorption while preventing backflow. It remains flexible, discreet, and almost weightless, offering reliable protection without thickness or noise.

5

Patented Suspension Architecture

A unique floating design helps maintain the pad's form during movement, reducing friction while preventing side leakage. This adaptive structure adjusts naturally with body position and underwear fit for secure coverage throughout the day.

6

Antibacterial Protection System

A clean, holistic antibacterial strategy combines tea polyphenols, silver ions, and negative ions to inhibit bacterial growth, break down odor molecules, and preserve a fresh, hygienic environment—especially during heavier days. No harsh chemicals, no artificial scents.

7

360° Sealed Sterile Wrapping

Every pad is protected inside a completely sealed wrapper, safeguarding it against moisture, dust, and airborne contaminants. Each piece arrives perfectly clean, fresh, and ready for use—a small but essential detail for maintaining intimate hygiene.
