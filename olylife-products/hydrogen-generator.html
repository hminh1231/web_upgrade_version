Overview

The Olylife Hydrogen Generator is an innovative device powered by SPE (Solid Polymer Electrolyte) electrolysis technology. It produces a mixture of pure hydrogen and oxygen gas. Inhalation of this mixture is associated with potential benefits such as antioxidation and fatigue relief, among others.

Because hydrogen molecules are among the smallest in nature, they can penetrate cell membranes more readily than many other substances. The device is designed to support the delivery of hydrogen to tissues and to potentially influence cellular processes as part of a comprehensive wellness approach.

Functions

The Olylife Hydrogen Generator operates at a flow rate of 666 ml/min for hydrogen and 333 ml/min for oxygen, generating hydrogen through electrolysis with a concentration of 99.9%. Beyond its role as a potent antioxidant, it may help address fatigue, promote quality sleep, boost metabolism, and support a balanced immune system. By maintaining an optimal 2:1 ratio for combined hydrogen–oxygen inhalation, the generator is designed as a comprehensive solution for overall well-being.
